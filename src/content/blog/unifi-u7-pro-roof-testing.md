# UniFi U7 Pro Roof-Mounted Wi-Fi Testing on a Rural Property

This article will document real-world testing of a UniFi U7 Pro installed high on a rural property roof.

The goal is to test whether a roof-mounted access point can provide useful outdoor coverage across sheds, yards, driveways and nearby property areas.

## What I Want to Test

- Practical outdoor range
- Speed at different distances
- Performance through walls and sheds
- Roaming behaviour with existing UniFi access points
- Device stability
- Whether roof mounting is worth the effort

## Test Areas

Planned test locations include:

- Around the house
- Driveway
- Shed area
- Gate approach
- Water tank area
- Paddock-facing side of the property
